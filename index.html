Food Heaven Restaurant is a very famous restaurant because of its location next to the beach and also because of its modern European style cuisines, with an experience accentuated by the high quality of service provided. The Asia Pacific Management Forum had voted the place as the best restaurant in Asia where guests can have a late but warm breakfast, a nice lunch by the sea and a candlelit dinner along with a few drinks with friends at night in a romantic setting. Neither the sand nor the stars seem too far away. The restaurant is consistently voted amongst the best in all restaurants in terms of food quality and the pricing on the menu..
